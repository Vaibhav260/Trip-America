New York City — 9 Days Spread 🗽

Here's the best way to distribute your NYC highlights across 9 days:

🌳

Day 1 — Central Park + Uptown

Central Park walk, Bethesda Fountain, Bow Bridge, The Met Museum (on 5th), Roosevelt Island tram.

🌆

Day 2 — Midtown Icons

Top of the Rock (Rockefeller Center), Times Square, The Vessel, Broadway show tickets.

⛴️

Day 3 — Statue of Liberty

Take the Staten Island Ferry (FREE, best views!). Or book ferry to Liberty Island. Ellis Island included.

🌉

Day 4 — Brooklyn Day

Walk Brooklyn Bridge (start Manhattan side), DUMBO neighborhood, Brooklyn Bridge Park, sideline views, Jane's Carousel.

🏛️

Day 5 — Lower Manhattan

9/11 Memorial & Museum, Wall Street, Charging Bull, Oculus (WTC Transit Hub), Battery Park.

🎭

Day 6 — Culture Day

MoMA (Museum of Modern Art — paid, but incredible), Washington Square Park, Flatiron Building, Harry Potter NYC store.

🧲

Day 7 — Mercer Labs + Discoveries

Mercer Labs (immersive art), New York Public Library, Grand Central Terminal, High Line park.

🎨

Day 8 — Free Friday Art

Brooklyn Museum (Free on Fridays evenings 5-11PM!), Madame Tussauds, Greenwich Village dinner.

🛍️

Day 9 — Final Day

Last-minute shopping (SoHo, Canal St), Akshardham Temple NJ (if desired, 45 min drive), evening departure prep.

🗽 NYC Practical Tips • MTA Subway: Get an OMNY card (tap to pay) or use your phone. Unlimited 7-day pass ~$34.
• Statue of Liberty: Staten Island Ferry is FREE and gives great views. Actual island visit costs ~$24 + book MONTHS ahead for crown access.
• Top of the Rock: Better views of Empire State Building than Empire State itself. Book online to save time.
• Broadway: Check TKTS booth in Times Square for 50% off same-day tickets!
• Free fun: Brooklyn Bridge walk, Staten Island Ferry, all Smithsonians, Central Park, High Line, Washington Square Park — all free!
• Akshardham: In Robbinsville, NJ (~45 min from Manhattan). Completely free but no phones inside.
