World-class FREE museum. Da Vinci, Vermeer, Monet, Rembrandt, Picasso. West Building = classics, East Building = modern. Underground tunnel between them is a neon art installation. Closes 5 PM — arrive by 2:45 PM for max time.

TKTS booth in Times Square for 40–50% off same-day Broadway tickets. Brooklyn Museum free on Friday evenings 5–11 PM. Akshardham (NJ) — closed Tuesdays, no cameras allowed inside.

Akshardham NJ + Grand Central + Library
  • 9:00 AM — Drive to BAPS Akshardham, Robbinsville NJ (car day! 45 min drive)
  • 10:00 AM — Akshardham: one of the largest Hindu temples in the Western Hemisphere. FREE! No phones inside. Allow 3–4 hrs. Closed Tuesdays.
  • 2:00 PM — Lunch in Edison/Iselin NJ — Little India! Best biryani outside India.
  • 4:00 PM — Drive back, park car again
  • 5:30 PM — New York Public Library (Rose Main Reading Room is stunning — free)
  • 7:00 PM — Grand Central Terminal (Whispering Gallery, Celestial Ceiling — free)
  • 8:30 PM — Dinner in Koreatown or Midtown East
  • 10:00 PM — Bryant Park evening (behind the library, lit up at night)
🧲
